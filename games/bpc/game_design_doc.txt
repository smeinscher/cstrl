Beer Pong Champion

Introduction
    Game Summary
        A beer pong game with progression of certain skills, akin to NBA 2k MyCareer mode
    Inspiration
        Punch Club
        NBA 2k
    Player Experience
        The player must learn optimal shooting mechanics and strategies for beating each opponent.
        The player will increase stats after each game
    Platform
        Windows/Linux
    Development Software
        Custom game framework
        Aseprite
        Ableton
    Genre
        Singleplayer, fighting, rpg
    Target Audience
        ???

Concept
    The player takes control of a college freshmen who was introduced to a secret beer pong league, setup
    by country elites for morally questionable entertainment. The beer pong team follows the college football
    team for cover.
